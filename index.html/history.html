Our History

The Black Pine was founded in 1984 by local food enthusiasts who had a passion for traditional BBQ and pizza with a modern twist. Over the years, we've grown into a beloved part of the community, known for our slow-smoked BBQ and creative pizza recipes. Our restaurant is a place where memories are made and where great food brings people together.

Key Milestones

  • 1984: The Black Pine opens its doors.
  • 1990: Expanded to include our signature smoked brisket.
  • 2000: Introduced a new range of creative pizzas that became instant hits.
  • 2020: Rebranded with a focus on quality, sustainability, and local produce.
